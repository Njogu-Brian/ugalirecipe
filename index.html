Delicious Ugali Mayai



This is a simple recipe to make a delicious plate of Ugali Mayai accompanied by avocado



Ingridients

  • 2 cups of water
  • 1 cup of maize floor
  • 2 eggs
  • pinch of salt
  • 2 table spoons of cooking oil
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 onion
  • 1 table spoon paprika
  • black peper
  • soy sauce
  • coriander
  • avocado

Instructions

  1. Add water to the sufuria and boil
  2. Add Maize floor to the boiling water and stir till ishikane
  3. Let it cook for 5 minitues under low heat
  4. Flip the ugali to the other side to cook evenly then remove it from the sufuria
  5. Cut the onions and tomatoes into small piecies into a bowl
  6. Break the eggs into a bowl and which them to mix well
  7. Add salt and black peper to the egg mixture and stir
  8. Preheat a frying pan and add cooking oil
  9. Pour the mixture into the pan and cook till they reach your desired constitency
  10. Add paprika and a little soy source and stir for a minute
  11. Add corriander to the pan then remove it
  12. Serve the dish in a plate alongside the hot ugali and a slice of avocado
